About Us

The Mary Emmanson Foundation is a non-profit organisation registered to work in partnership with individuals, organizations and government to bring education, healthcare and innovation to those in need through development projects.

  • Vision: A thriving, educated, innovative and healthy society.
  • Mission: Deliver practical projects in partnership with all stakeholders through education, healthcare and innovation.

Our Programs

Education Support

School materials, scholarships and teacher training for rural schools. Our NGO is dedicated to transforming lives through access to quality education. We believe that every individual, regardless of background, deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and reach their full potential. Through targeted programs, teacher training, and community engagement, we work to bridge educational gaps and empower learners of all ages. By providing resources, mentorship, and innovative learning methods, we strive to create a future where education is a powerful tool for change and equality.

Healthcare

Community health outreach, screenings and maternal care support. Improving health outcomes is at the heart of our mission. We focus on expanding access to healthcare, promoting preventive measures, and supporting vulnerable communities. Our initiatives include health education campaigns, medical outreach, and partnerships with local clinics and hospitals. By addressing both immediate needs and long-term health challenges, we aim to build healthier, more resilient communities where everyone has the chance to live a life of dignity and well-being.

Innovation

Introducing new approach to solving daily challenges through innovative ways. Innovation is the engine of progress. We champion creative solutions to social challenges by supporting entrepreneurship, fostering collaboration, and investing in new ideas. Our projects connect visionaries with resources, mentorship, and networks to turn bold concepts into real-world impact. From technology-driven health solutions to novel approaches in education, we empower changemakers to shape a brighter, more inclusive future for all.
